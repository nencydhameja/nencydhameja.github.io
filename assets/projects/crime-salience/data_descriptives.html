Data sources

Crime: Chicago Police Department (CPD) incident-level reported crimes, 2005–2021. Geocoded to latitude/longitude. Classified by FBI/IUCR code into violent, property, disorder, drug/vice, and domestic categories.

Sales: Cook County Assessor repeat-sales transactions, matched to parcel identifiers. Restricted to arm’s-length residential sales.

Geography: Crimes linked to properties via distance rings (0–0.05, 0.05–0.10, …, 0.40–0.50 mi). Time windows from 1 to 12 months before sale.

Temporal placebo: Post-sale crime (occurring after the sale date) serves as a placebo—if crime after a sale affects the price, the design has a problem.
